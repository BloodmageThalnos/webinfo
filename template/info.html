GM & Associates

GM & Associates (hereafter GMA) is a public policy and government affairs advisory firm with a global perspective and local experience established in Beijing, China in 2005.

With combined experience in the public and private sectors, our approach and advice are based on first-hand knowledge and deep insight into both the highly complex policy making environment and the competitive business world.

Over the past 15 years, we have counselled a wide range of international clients on their business-critical public policy and public affairs needs in China, providing recommendations rooted in direct dialogue with key stakeholders and a multi-dimensional understanding of the subject matter.

Our client industries include healthcare, medical devices, agriculture, biotech, forestry, industrial equipment, telecommunications, electronics, e-commerce and finance.

PA40 Forum

In December of 2014, we established the “Multinational Corporation’s Public Affairs Forum”, also known as the “PA40 Forum”, with 17 multinational enterprise (MNC) founding members in Beijing.

With GM & Associates functioning as its secretariat, the forum has grown to include 40 member companies and now serves as not only an essential platform that enables members to better understand China’s investment policies and business environment, but also as an avenue for communication with key policy stakeholders.
