GM & Associates (GMA) is a public affairs and government relations advisory firm established in 2005. We are a team of experienced consultants.

For years GMA was hired to conduct policy research and government affairs services for leading international clients in sectors such as agriculture, electronics, e-commerce, food, finance, healthcare, metal, paper, trade, etc.

Our core service includes policy research – to monitor, analyze and alert on policy updates; issues audit - to determine drivers behind the scenes; market entry - to research into policies restricting your business and identify solutions; issues & strategies - make strategies and action plans on critical policy issues; senior executive China visit support, etc.

If you need further information, please feel free to contact gma@gm-associates.cn.
