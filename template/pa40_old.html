PA40 {%if language_en %}Forum{%else%}俱乐部{%endif%}
PA40 Forum is a platform consisting of 40 globally well-known multinational corporations (MNCs), aimed at improving investment climate in China through both formal and informal communication. The secretariat is operated by GM & Associates (GMA) in Beijing.
For the past six years, the Forum has organized seminars on governmental policies and regulations, and assisted company members to have in-depth dialogues with key Chinese government agencies at central level. Our policy recommendations have received regular attention and were subsequently used in the formation of government directives from the central government.
The Beijing-based secretariat GMA was founded in 2005 and serves as a strategic consultancy for enterprises on public policy and governmental affairs. Throughout our years of service to our clients at home and abroad, GMA has accumulated substantial and broad experience from both international and local perspectives. The primary goal of GMA is to promote economic integration between China and the world.
